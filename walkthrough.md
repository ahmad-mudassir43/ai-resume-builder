# Resume Builder & Pro AI Features

The application has been upgraded with professional safeguards and flexibility!

## 1. Advanced Fail-Safes & Controls
- **Revert Magic:** The moment you click "Optimize", the app temporarily saves your entire past resume. Suppose the AI deletes a bullet point you loved—just click the red **"Revert"** button that appears below "Optimize", and your resume shifts back in time seamlessly.
- **Alternative Perspectives:** Don't like how the AI optimized your phrasing? Click **"Alternative"** next to Revert. This forcefully reruns the algorithm to come up with completely different synonyms and sentence structures.
- **Nuclear Reset:** In the top header next to settings, there is a red **Trash Icon**. Clicking this will explicitly ask you for confirmation, then safely nuke all entered fields—leaving you with a perfectly blank slate so you can quickly parse a different resume image without old artifacts lingering around.

## 2. Multi-Model Support (OpenAI GPT-4o integration)
You are no longer locked into just Google Gemini!

- Click the **Gear Settings Icon** at the top right.
- You will now see a sleek dropdown labeled **"AI Provider"**.
- You can freely toggle between **Google Gemini (2.5 Flash)** and **OpenAI (GPT-4o)**. It dynamically asks for the appropriate `sk-...` API key and routes all Image Parsing and Text Optimization through OpenAI's premium APIs if selected. (Your settings and keys safely persist in local storage).

## Best Workflow
1. Use **Gemini/OpenAI** to import your Image.
2. Read the newly extracted base fields in the Editor.
3. Paste a Job Description to the ATS Checker.
4. Click **Optimize**.
5. Read the Optimization Summary pop-up.
6. Not entirely thrilled? Click **Alternative**.
7. Want to write it yourself? Click **Revert**.
8. Click **Export PDF** to land your job!
